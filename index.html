📲 Save Any App to Your Home Screen

Every app here is mobile-friendly and can be saved as a shortcut — opens full screen, no browser bar, just like a native app.

🍎 iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. Open the app link in Safari — not Chrome, not Instagram/WhatsApp
  2. Tap the Share icon at the bottom (box with arrow pointing up)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Edit the name if you like, then tap Add
Tip: If you delete the shortcut, just reopen Safari, find the app in your history, and repeat. Nothing is lost — the app lives on the web.

🤖 Android (Chrome)

  1. Open the app link in Chrome
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots, top right)
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  4. Confirm by tapping Add
Shortcut: Some Android devices show an "Install app" banner at the bottom of Chrome — tap that for a one-step install.

💡 A Few Things Worth Knowing

Works offline? Some apps (like Panchang) are fully offline-capable once loaded. Others need an active connection.
No app store needed. These are web apps — nothing to download, nothing to update manually. Always the latest version.
Safe to delete. Removing the shortcut from your Home Screen doesn't delete anything. Just reinstall from the link anytime.
Best browser: Always use Safari on iPhone and Chrome on Android for the smoothest install experience.
