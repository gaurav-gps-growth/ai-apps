📲 Save Any App to Your Home Screen

Every app here is mobile-friendly and can be saved as a shortcut — opens full screen, no browser bar.

🍎 iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. Open the app link in Safari (not Chrome, not Instagram/WhatsApp)
  2. Tap the Share icon at the bottom (box with arrow pointing up)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Edit the name if you like, then tap Add

🤖 Android (Chrome)

  1. Open the app link in Chrome
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots, top right)
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  4. Confirm by tapping Add
